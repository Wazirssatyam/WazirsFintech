Welcome, Wazir's FinTech is a privately funded quantitative trading firm known for excellence in Algo Trading and financial markets' research.

Wazirs Trading Desk specializes in system-based trading strategies across various financial instruments. All the strategies are well-researched and back-tested on historical data to determine the viability and performance across various parameters and market phases.The execution is managed thru automated algos to have error-free order-flow. Most of the volume is done in Delta Neutral strategies along with some directional setups. Various data matrices are studied in real time and continuous improvements are made to improve the risk-reward and minimize the drawdowns.

Our Trading System Attributes and Risk Management

  • Current trading systems are focused on consistent returns with least possible drawdowns.
  • All accounts are monitored live by trading team.
  • No naked exposure.
  • No leverage.
  • Well-defined strategy and portfolio level SLs on daily/monthly TF.
  • OTM hedges maintained to safeguard from Black Swans.
  • Capital is kept in Liquid Bees/debt to boost returns.
  • Continuous data collection for improvements.
